Sales Dashboard

Overview

For a recent data analysis project, I developed a Sales Dashboard using Tableau to analyze and visualize sales performance. The objective of the project was to provide stakeholders with an interactive tool to track key performance indicators (KPIs), identify trends, and compare sales data across different product categories and geographical regions. My contributions included gathering and preparing the data from multiple sources, including sales, customer, and product data, and performing data modeling to establish relationships between them.

I used Tableau to create various visualizations, including bar-in-bar charts, sparklines, and KPIs, to display sales trends and comparisons effectively. I implemented interactive elements, such as dynamic filters and parameters, which allowed users to adjust the dashboard based on specific criteria like time periods and product categories. This ensured that stakeholders could conduct tailored analysis according to their needs.

Additionally, I focused on ensuring high data quality and accuracy by thoroughly cleaning and validating the datasets before connecting them to Tableau. This project helped me hone my skills in data visualization, data modeling, and user-centered design.
